RAS Talk on “Cutting Edge Applications of undergraduate control”  

IEEE RAS Student Branch chapter, IIT Mandi organised an online talk on “cutting edge applications of undergraduate control” on 7th November 2020. The talk was delivered by Dr. Ramprasad Potluri, associate professor at IIT Kanpur. The main highlights of the talk included discussion on topics including Moon rover, CSL and NCSL, Automatic book copier. The talk began around 12 pm(IST) and lasted for around 2 hrs with Q & A session in between the talk.
It was broadcasted (from SC, NKN, IIT Mandi) through the “Cisco Webex” virtual meeting platform.
Lecture Link: https://bit.ly/talk_iitmandi

IAS Distinguished Lecture:  

IEEE Student Branch Chapter Indian Institute of Technology Mandi,India conducted a Webinar on “Distinguished Lecture on condition monitoring and fault diagnosis of electric machines” to celebrate the IEEE PELS Day 2020, July27, 2020, at 3:30 pm by Prof. Jose A.Antonino-Daviu from Universitat Politecnica de Valencia, Valencia, Spain. This topic is not just in a hot demand of the industries but also has vital role in supporting the current infrastructure that depends mostly on the electrical machineries. So, we have to incorporate the ideas of monitoring and its fault diagnosis for machines to make an industry run without any discontinuity.
It was broadcasted (from SC, NKN, IIT Mandi) through the “Cisco Webex” virtual meeting platform.

HARDWARE HACKATHON:  HACKTRONICS

Hacktronics is a Hardware Hackathon being organised by Aavesh Electronics Association of IIIT Una,in collaboration with the IEEE Student Branch, IIT Mandi.
The 48 hour hackathon is to be conducted on 11th and 12th April,where students can participate in teams to design and build some innovative hardware project over a period of two days.
Hacktronics 2020 has been thought to be an event with no restrictions on themes and projects under all categories can be registered.

Workshop

The IEEE PELS SB Chapter, IIT Mandi is organizing a two days' workshop on Raspberry Pi on March 7-8th, 2020 (Venue will be notified soon). The workshop will cover basic to advance level concept on the Raspberry Pi development board though the application in the Internet of Things (IoT) and Home automation.
The workshop is open to all. A certificate will be provided after successful completion of the course.
